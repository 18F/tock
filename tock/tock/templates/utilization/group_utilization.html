Utilization by Unit

This report contains active billable 18F employees by their current business unit.

Utilization is calculated by dividing the total number of hours submitted on projects that are marked "billable" in Tock, divided by the total number of hours submitted on all projects for the given period. Out-of-Office project lines are excluded from all calculations.

This is the latest iteration in utilization reporting and an active work in progress. If an employee is missing, the numbers seem off, or you have any questions/concerns please don't hesitate to reach out in #tock-dev
